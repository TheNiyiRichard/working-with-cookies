A cookie is a small piece of text sent to your browser by a website you can visit. It helps the site remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit again and make the site more useful to you. Cookies are part of the HTTP protocol. They are usually set by a web-server using the response 'Set-Cookie' HTTP header.

This website uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Click "Accept All" to allow all cookies or "Settings" to customize your preferences.
